Housing the Homeless of California Fund

The Statistics of this crisis

  • 24% of homeless people in the USA live in California
  • There are over 135,000 homeless in California
  • In San Francisco, 13% of homeless are working

What we want to do

  • We want to fund the building of permanent housing for homeless
  • These apartments will be built stricly for people who cannot find a stable home
  • For the first year, these apartments will be free to the people living in them for the first year, then they will be charged a small fee
  • Money made from those fees will be put towards maintaining the building, and funding more apartments
